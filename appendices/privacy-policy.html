Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how MotionInput Games uses any personal data collected when you use the AI-AutoStart and SoundLab Games applications.

What data do we collect?

MotionInput Games collects the following data:

  • Gesture configuration settings (gesture mappings, application and game paths)
  • Player settings and preferences
  • Game progress and story data
  • Webcam frames processed in real time for gesture recognition

No raw video, images, or biometric data are stored.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide MotionInput Games with all of the data we collect. We collect and process data when you:

  • Enable gesture recognition and use the webcam
  • Configure gestures and application mappings
  • Play the SoundLab Game and save progress
  • Enter prompts or story content in the game

All data is processed locally on your device.

How will we use your data?

MotionInput Games collects your data so that we can:

  • Detect hand gestures and trigger desktop automation (AI-AutoStart)
  • Control applications, games, and files through gesture recognition
  • Store player settings and game progress (SoundLab Games)
  • Provide interactive gameplay and local story generation

The data is used only to ensure proper functioning of the application.

How do we store your data?

MotionInput Games stores your data locally on your device.

  • Configuration files are saved locally
  • Game progress and settings are stored locally
  • Webcam frames are processed in real time and not stored
  • No data is sent to external servers or third parties

We never have remote access to your data.

Users can delete their data at any time by removing configuration or progress files or uninstalling the application.

What are your data protection rights?

MotionInput Games would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

The right to erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request restriction of data processing under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to data processing under certain conditions.

The right to data portability: You have the right to request transfer of your data to another organisation or directly to you.

Since all data is stored locally, users can exercise these rights by deleting or modifying their local data files.

Camera and Gesture Recognition

AI-AutoStart and SoundLab Games use a webcam for real-time gesture recognition.

  • Camera access starts only when the user enables gesture recognition
  • Frames are processed locally
  • No video or images are stored
  • No biometric identification is performed
  • Camera usage can be disabled at any time

This ensures transparency and user control over sensitive input.

MotionInput Games keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this page.
